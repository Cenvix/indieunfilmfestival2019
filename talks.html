Clodualdo “Doy” del Mundo Jr. is a highly respected figure in the Philippine film industry, having written Manila Sa Kuko Ng Liwanag (1975), and directed Pepot Artista (2005), Paglipad ng Anghel (2011,) and many more.

Clodualdo del Mundo Jr.

Antoinette “Tonette” Jadaone is a filmmaker, writer and producer, a few of her works being That Thing Called Tadhana (2014) and teleserye On The Wings Of Love (2015).

Antoinette Jadaone

Pepe Diokno is a motion picture director, producer and screenwriter. His notable films include Above The Clouds (2016) and Kapatiran (2015).

Pepe Diokno

Ice Idanan has been recognized locally and internationally for her work such as Limang Libo (2009) and Sakaling Hindi Makarating (2016).

Ice Idanan

At only 17, Jared Joven has proven his skill in filmmaking by winning an award for best film at the Shanghai Queer Film Festival last 2016 together with his co-director Kaj Palanca for their film entitled Contestant #4.

Jared Joven
